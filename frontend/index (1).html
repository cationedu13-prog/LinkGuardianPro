How it works

Enterprise-grade monitoring
at indie prices

Three detection layers ensure nothing slips through — even for bot-protected sites.

🌐
Layer 1 – Direct HTTP
Fast direct request with custom headers. Catches 90% of broken links and out-of-stock products instantly under 15 seconds.
🔄
Layer 2 – ScraperAPI
Renders JavaScript-heavy pages like Amazon and Flipkart that block direct requests. Uses real browser fingerprints.
🎭
Layer 3 – Playwright
Full headless Chromium browser as the final fallback. Handles dynamic AJAX-loaded stock status perfectly.
📱
Telegram Alerts
Instant alerts the moment a link breaks or goes out of stock. No spam — one alert per status change, delivered in under 1 second.
🏷
Out-of-Stock Detection
Specialized keyword patterns for Amazon, Flipkart, Shopify, and generic e-commerce. Detects "Sold Out", "Currently Unavailable", and more.
📊
Check History
90-day rolling history of every check. See response times, detect patterns, and audit exactly when a link changed status.
